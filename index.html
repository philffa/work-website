How this is priced

Soil and bulk material jobs are priced by volume moved, not by the hour — so you get certainty on cost and I'm rewarded for working efficiently.

  • Flat ground, short carry: ~$65–70/m³
  • Steep or long carry: ~$72–80/m³
  • Stairs or difficult access: ~$82–90/m³
  • Minimum call-out: $80

How this is priced

Demolition is quoted by structure size and type. Tip costs are passed on at cost for Brisbane runs; Gatton tip ($17/load) is included in Lockyer Valley jobs.

  • Timber shed: from $350
  • Large shed: from $500 (excl. slab)
  • Concrete slab: priced by volume + removal
  • Asbestos: not currently offered

How this is priced

Tip runs are charged per load for simplicity — no weighbridge surprises. Gatton tip fees ($17/load) are included. Brisbane tip fees are passed on at cost.

  • Gatton/Lockyer tip: all-inclusive per load
  • Brisbane tip: load fee + actual tip charges
  • Loading/unloading included
  • Minimum call-out: $80
How this is priced

Turf laying is priced by area. Clients usually supply their own turf — I'll lay it efficiently and cleanly. I can also source and supply turf if preferred.

  • Labour: ~$12–24/m² depending on prep
  • Turf supply: market rate passed on
  • Ground prep quoted separately if needed
  • Minimum call-out: $80
How this is priced

Garden jobs are estimated by size, scope and current condition. A full clean of a neglected large block takes significantly more effort than a routine tidy.

  • Small tidy: from $90
  • Medium block full service: $280–420
  • Large neglected: $500+
  • All estimates include cleanup & disposal
